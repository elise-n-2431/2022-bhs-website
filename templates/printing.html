Printing:

Method One- Personal Computer:

If you don't have printing credit, purchase credit first
1. Log into the papercut website
2. Go to the 'Web Print' header
3. Select 'Submit a Job'

4. Choose a printer

5. Choose your options
6. Upload your pdf file

Method Two- School Computer:

1. Go to the file which you want to print
2. Ctrl+P to bring up the print menu
3. Customise the settings as required and select the printer: “____” for black and white prints or “____” for colour
4. Select print
5. Confirm the charges to the papercut account

Photocopying:

1. Go to the machine in the library with the label: photocopier
2. Lift up the lid and place your paper face down, aligned with the top left corner
3. Enter your student number and your pin (the numbers from your student passsword)
4. Tap on “student account”
5. Select “copy” on the main screen
6. Customise the settings as required
7. Press the big green button to start

Add credit to your account:

The cost to print at burnside for either A4 or A3 is 3 cents in black and white or 20 cents in colour.
Your account needs to have printing credit to cover the cost of the printing.
You can check your balance on the papercut website or it will be displayed at the top right hand corner of school computers.
To top up this credit you can go to the student office and purchase a card for either $2 or $5.
This card has numbers on it which you can enter into the papercut website under the top up header.

Common Errors:

You need printing credit to print, look above for instructions
